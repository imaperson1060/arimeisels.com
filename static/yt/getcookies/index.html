How to get YouTube Cookies

NEVER share your cookies with anyone. If they have this, they can pretend to be you on Google services. We will never give out your cookies, and this website is open-source, so you can confirm that.


1. Go onto a YouTube video that you have access to but the general public won't (i.e. private, premium, age restricted)

2. Right click the page and inspect elements

3. Go to the "Network" tab

4. Reload the page to populate it

5. Select the first item (or the first appearance of the video id)

6. Scroll down to "Request Headers"

7. Right click the "Cookie" entry and click Copy Value

8. Begin the download process

9. When prompted, paste the cookie string you copied

10. Enjoy your download!
