youtube cookies

never share your cookies with anyone. if anyone has them, they can pretend to be you on google services. i will never give out your cookies, and this website is open-source, so you can confirm that.

when are user cookies required?

some youtube videos may be private or age restricted. these videos cannot be downloaded without an authenticated user. providing user cookies enables full functionality. you will be alerted if user cookies are required to download a video.

how to get user cookies:

note: these instructions may vary between browsers

1. go onto a youtube video
2. right click the page and inspect to open developer tools
3. go to the "network" tab
4. reload the page (ctrl + r)
5. select the first item (or the first appearance of the video id, as shown below)
6. scroll down to "request headers"
7. copy the entirety of the cookie header
8. go back, and paste the cookie string into the appropriate field
9. press load video
